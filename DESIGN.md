# Nova Empire Design System

## Global Visual Style
**Graphic Noir Futurism**.
- The overall aesthetic is dark, industrial, and futuristic.
- Elements should feature frosted glass effects (blur/translucency).
- Use neon borders for accents and active states.
- Incorporate half-tone overlays and static noise for texture.
- UI elements should look like they are bolted to a dashboard, with "mountingBolts" (industrial bolts) at the corners.

## Colors
- **Background**: Extremely dark, `#111316`.
- **Primary / Accents**: Neon colors (e.g., bright cyan, neon red, vibrant orange, or neon green) depending on context, maintaining a gritty sci-fi look.
- **Surfaces**: Dark grey or translucent layers to create depth.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Inter` for clear readability on UI elements.
- **Display Font**: `Rajdhani` (or a similar futuristic condensed font) for headers, numbers, and important labels.
- Text should have high contrast against the dark backgrounds (white or light grey).

## Components
- **Buttons**: Angular, industrial, potentially with neon borders or glow on hover/active.
- **Panels**: Frosted glass look, sometimes with visible mounting bolts in the corners.
- **Icons**: Minimalist, technical, sci-fi themed.
